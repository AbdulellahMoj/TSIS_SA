# Cold Message Templates

All templates are starting points. Every message must be personalised
with at least one specific, researched detail before sending.

Rules:
- Under 150 words ideal. Never over 250.
- No filler: not "I hope this finds you well"
- One specific hook showing real research
- Two credentials maximum — pick the strongest for this audience
- Soft ask — never "hire me"
- End with your name

---

## Template A — To CEO or Founder

```
Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name] — [Year] CS student at [University], [top credential 1] and [top credential 2].

I've been following [Company]'s work on [specific thing from research — a product, a paper, a recent announcement].

A bit about me:
- [Most relevant project — one sentence with a metric or outcome]
- [Most relevant credential or experience]

Looking for a summer internship starting [Month Year]. Would it be okay to send my CV?

[Your Name]
```

```

[Your Name]
```

## Template D — To Employee (warm — you already have context on them)

```
Hi [Name],

I came across your [post about X / work on Y / talk at Z] — [one sentence showing you genuinely engaged with it].

I'm [Your Name], [Year] CS student at [University]. I'm targeting [Company] for a summer internship and found your background really relevant to what I'm working on.

[One sentence connecting your work to theirs.]

Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call? I'd value your perspective.

[Your Name]
```

## Template F — Follow-Up (no reply after 7–10 days)

```
Hi [Name],

Following up on my message from [date / "last week"].

Still very interested in [Company] — happy to share more work if useful.

[Your Name]
```

---

## Personalisation Checklist

Every message must have at least ONE of:

- [ ] Something specific about their recent post, project, or announcement
- [ ] A connection between the company's tech or domain and something you built
- [ ] A shared context (university, program, event, research area, Saudi ecosystem)

If none apply: research more before sending.

---

## What Not to Do

❌ "I hope this message finds you well."
❌ "I am reaching out because I am very interested in your company."
❌ "I believe I would be a great fit for your team."
❌ Three paragraphs before saying who you are.
❌ Listing every credential you have.
❌ Asking to "hop on a call" with zero context.
❌ Sending the same message to 20 people.
